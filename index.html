Path to Liberation

Beliefs and Legacy

Rooted in ancient wisdom, Jainism teaches that liberation is attained through non-violence, truth, and self-discipline. Guided by the teachings of the Tirthankaras, seekers strive to free the soul from the cycle of birth and karma.

A Jain should live with compassion, self-restraint, and devotion to the path of liberation.

Beyond the noise of the material world, in the silence of inner discipline, live the souls who seek truth, peace, and freedom from karma through right knowledge, right faith, and right conduct.

Pandit Todarmal Smarak Trust

Connect, Grow and Serve with Us

With holy inspiration of great spiritual personality Shree Kanjiswami, Shree Todarmal Smarak Bhavan was built by Jaipur's businessman Shree Puranchand Godika to disseminate Jain Tattvagyan (metaphysics) and founded Pandit Todarmal Smarak Trust.

He was the founder Chairman of the trust, now his worthy son Shree Sushilkumar Godika is taking care as the Chairman of the trust. It's worth recall the remarkable contribution put by Pandit Nemichand Patni, who worked as General Secretary for the promotion of the trust right from its formation. Right from the date of inception and till date, under the guidance of great Scholar & Philosopher, Tatvavetta Dr. Hukamchand Bharill, trust has been actively and continuously doing all activities and fullfilling the dreams of the Great founder of this trust.

Educational Programmes

Siddhant Mahavidyalaya

Institution: Shri Todarmal Di. Jain Siddhant Mahavidyalaya Focus Areas: Jain Philosophy, Spirituality, Scriptural Studies Established to Preserve & Propagate Jinwani

The Digambar Jain community has long felt the absence of authoritative scholars in Jain philosophy and spirituality. To fulfill this need, Shri Todarmal Di. Jain Siddhant Mahavidyalaya was founded with the sole mission of nurturing true propagators of Jinwani.

Todarmal Mukt Vidyapeeth

Institution: Shri Todarmal Digambar Jain Siddhant Mahavidyalaya Focus Areas: Spiritual Education, Jain Philosophy, Personality Development Empowering Minds through Jinwani

Spiritual education plays a vital role in shaping an individual's character and inner growth. Shri Todarmal Mukt Vidyapeeth is a unique institution devoted to nurturing wisdom, discipline, and compassion through the eternal principles of Jain philosophy.

Pareeksha Board

Institution: Shri Veetrag Vigyan Vidyapeeth Focus Areas: Moral Values, Youth Education, Jain Ethics Established in 1968

Shri Veetrag Vigyan Vidyapeeth Pareeksha Board was founded in 1968 with the goal of promoting moral awareness among children and youth. We are proud to say the Vidyapeeth has remained steadfast in its mission since inception, instilling Jain values through structured educational assessments.
